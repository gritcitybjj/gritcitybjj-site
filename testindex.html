Code of Conduct

Code of Conduct

  • 01. Always be respectful.
  • 02. Always address Professors and Coaches properly.
  • 03. Uphold our principles on and off the mats.
  • 04. If late, wait outside until permitted.
  • 05. Bow on and off the mats.
  • 06. No leaving mat area without permission.
  • 07. Maintain posture during instruction.
  • 08. Talking minimal and relevant only.
  • 09. No foul language.
  • 10. Be humble.
  • 11. Believe & Achieve.

Etiquette

  • No shoes on mats.
  • Bottled drinks only.
  • No electronics in training area.
  • Change only in designated areas.
  • Gear stored properly.

Hygiene

  • Maintain hygiene.
  • Uniform clean.
  • Trim nails.
  • No jewelry.

Uniform

  • Academy patches required.
  • No other school patches allowed.

Spectators

  • Remain in designated seating.
  • Devices silent.
  • No coaching students.
